# Video Business Plan

ProofPitch should be priced around completed demo-video output, not around API usage.

## Near-Term Packaging

- Free trial: limited captions-only renders.
- Founder: saved video projects and configured voiceover.
- Team: shared organization workspace and review workflow.
- Agency: higher render volume and client workspaces.

## What Must Be True Before Monetization

- Rendering is reliable in production.
- Supabase storage and signed URLs are stable.
- Voiceover failures are explainable and recoverable.
- Users can review and regenerate narration without support.
