# Market Positioning

ProofPitch is not a general presentation tool. The wedge is narrower: fast generated product demo videos for teams that need a clean narrated walkthrough.

## Buyer

- Founder-led B2B teams.
- Agencies producing launch or sales demo assets.
- Accelerators preparing cohorts for demos.

## Differentiation

- The URL is the source of the visual workflow.
- Internal claim filtering keeps unsupported claims out of narration.
- Captions-only fallback keeps output usable without voice provider setup.
- The product emits an MP4, not a pile of copy fragments.
